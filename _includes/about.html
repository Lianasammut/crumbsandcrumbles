About

Good old-fashioned homebaked goods. From me to you.

Hello! I am Liana Powell, the lucky owner of Crumbs & Crumbles.

I bake different varieties of party cakes, muffins, cookies and brownies. In fact that's how it works. You choose what you like and when you would like it and I take care of the rest!
